## Quantitative Research Paper
**Project:** The Role of Social Support in Mitigating PTSD-Related Sleep Disturbances.
* **Overview:** Designed a 2x2 factorial study to investigate how social support moderates the relationship between PTSD severity and sleep quality.
* **Analysis:** Utilized R Studio for factorial ANOVA; analyzed interactions between severity and support.
* **Coursework:** [Research Methods in Psychology: PSYC 3111](https://degreedraft.com/cu-boulder/courses/psyc-3111-psychological-science-2-research-methods-in-psychology)
