# Security Policy

I take the security of my personal projects and research data seriously. If you have identified a potential security vulnerability in this portfolio or any of the projects linked here, please report it via the following method:

### Reporting a Vulnerability
Please email me directly at: **piperbutts@gmail.com**

### What to include in your report:
*   A clear, concise description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce the issue.
*   The impact of the vulnerability (if known).

### What to expect:
*   **Acknowledgement:** I will confirm receipt of your report within 48 hours.
*   **Update:** I will provide a timeline for addressing the issue within 5 business days.
*   **Resolution:** If the vulnerability is verified, I will prioritize a fix and notify you once it has been deployed.

*Thank you for helping me keep my work secure.*
